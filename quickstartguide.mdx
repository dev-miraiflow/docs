---
title: "Quickstart Guide"
description: "Explore entire assessment workflow in under 10 minutes"
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1">
    **Visit the Website**

    - Go to the Miraiflow website at https://www.miraiflow.tech/.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2">
    **Sign Up**:

    1. Click on the **Sign UP** button located at the bottom right corner of the homepage.
    2. Fill out the **Signup Form** with a valid email address and password.
    3. Submit the form to create your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3">
    **Login**:

    1. For subsequent visits, use the **SignIn Form** with your registered email and password to log in
    2. Upon successful login, you will be directed to **My Dashboard**, which is the default page.

    <Info>
      All pages will redirect to **My Dashboard** untill there is an active assessment.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 4">
    **Create an Assessment**:

    1. On your dashboard, click on the **Create Assessment** button.
    2. Enter your desired assessment title in the provided field.

    <Info>
      You shall see a row item created in your dashboard with the same assessment title.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 5">
    **Assessment Info Page**:

    1. You will be redirected to the **Assessment Info Page**.
    2. Fill in all required details in the fields presented.
    3. Click on the **Save and Continue** button at the bottom of the page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 6">
    **Define Target Profile**:

    1. After submission, you will navigate to the **Define Target Profile Page**.
    2. Here, you can select applicable process groups by toggling process group cards on or off.
    3. All processes are enabled by default, set to Target Capability Level 2. You can change all to Level 1 using the **Set all to Level 1** button or keep them at Level 2.
    4. If necessary, disable any specific process by selecting N/A from the dropdown next to that process.
    5. Once you have made your selections, click on the **Save & Continue Button**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 7">
    1. **Assessment Planning**:
       - You will be taken to the **Assessment Planning Page**.
       - Enter a start date for your assessment.
  </Step>
</Steps>
